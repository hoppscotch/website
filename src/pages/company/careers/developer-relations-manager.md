# Developer Relations Manager

<span class="inline-flex px-4 py-2 text-xs rounded-full bg-accent text-accentContrast">Location: Remote</span>

### Overview

Hoppscotch is actively looking for a confident and empathetic individual to join our team as a developer relations manager. Your ultimate goal for this job position is to get new customers and retain existing customers.

As a developer relations manager, you will be responsible for maintaining a long-term relationship with the customer. You will also be responsible for knowing the customer’s needs and demands.

If you have persuasive abilities with highly creative ideas, we would like to meet you.

### Summary

You should be upselling as well as cross-selling the company’s products and services. As an ideal candidate, you should possess excellent communication skills and the ability to work in a team environment. In addition to this, you should have previous experience working as a developer relations manager.

As a developer relationship manager, your responsibilities include:

- Arranging conferences and gatherings with users
- Enlightening the customers about our serviceability
- Assisting customers in making the right choice

You will also play a vital role in developing advanced business procedures and recognizing facilities for sales.

### Responsibilities

- Setting customer expectations and revenue goals
- Identifying customer demands and requirements
- Developing plans to address customer-specific needs
- Dealing with people of different backgrounds and temperaments
- Solving customer issues and complaints
- Planning strategies for customer care and conflict resolution
- Cross-selling products and services to the customer
- Advising customers on cost-effectiveness
- Overseeing the activities of the sales team
- Offering exceptional customer service to customers
- Negotiating agreements with customers
- Maintaining good relationships with customers
- Approaching prospective customers to build rapport
- Taking feedback from customers to ensure they are satisfied with the service
- Adhering to all the rules and regulations of the company
- Researching the competitors and devising plans accordingly
- Staying updated with the latest ways to retain customers

### Qualifications

- 3 years of experience working as a developer relations manager or a similar role
- Demonstrate the ability to work in teams and independently
- Excellent organizational and interpersonal skills
- Outstanding oral and written communication skills
- Good customer service skills
- Exceptional negotiation, sales, and analytical skills
- Sound knowledge of the company’s products and services
- Ability to maintain sensitive and confidential information
- Excellent time management and presentation skills
- Ability to maintain a positive attitude
- Exceptional people and problem-solving skills
- Engineer mindset
